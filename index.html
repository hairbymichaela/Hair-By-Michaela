About Me

Hi, I’m Michaela.

I’m a freelance hairdresser based in Cheshunt, passionate about creating beautiful hair that makes you look and feel your best.

After working in the same salon from the age of 17, I took the leap and started Hair by Michaela—creating a business that reflects the experience I want every client to have.

Whether you’re ready for a full colour change or simply want your hair to feel like you again, you’ll always receive honest advice, careful attention and no pressure.

Michaela x

Before Your Appointment

A few important details.

To help your appointment run as smoothly as possible, please read the information below before booking.

◇

Colour services

A skin test is required at least 48 hours before all colour services. Unfortunately, a colour appointment cannot go ahead without a valid skin test.

○

Booking fee

A 20% non-refundable booking fee is required to secure all appointments. It will be deducted from the total cost of your service.

⌚

Cancellations

Please provide at least 48 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or rearrange your appointment.
